Shipping & Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Shipping Policy

Delivery Coverage

We deliver solar kits across all states and union territories of India through our reliable logistics partners.

Shipping Timeline

  • Metro Cities: 5-7 business days
  • Tier 2 Cities: 7-10 business days
  • Remote Locations: 10-15 business days
  • Bulk Orders: Timeline specified in quotation (typically 15-30 days)

Note: Timelines are estimates and may vary based on product availability, order size, and delivery location. Custom configurations may require additional time.

Shipping Charges

  • Shipping costs are calculated based on order weight, volume, and destination
  • For bulk orders above â‚¹5 lakhs, free shipping is available to select locations
  • Shipping charges will be clearly mentioned in the quotation
  • GST applicable as per current rates

Order Processing

  1. Order confirmation upon payment receipt
  2. Kit assembly and quality check (2-3 days)
  3. Secure packaging and documentation
  4. Handover to logistics partner
  5. Real-time tracking information shared via email/SMS

Delivery Requirements

  • Authorized person must be present to receive the shipment
  • Valid ID proof and delivery note acknowledgment required
  • For large shipments, appropriate unloading facilities required at site
  • Delivery address must be accurate and complete

Inspection on Delivery

Important: Please inspect all packages immediately upon delivery:

  • Check for external damage to packaging
  • Verify quantity of boxes/pallets received
  • Note any visible damage on the delivery receipt
  • Take photographs if damage is observed
  • Report issues within 48 hours of delivery

Shipment Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive:

  • Shipping confirmation email with tracking details
  • Logistics partner name and contact information
  • AWB/LR number for tracking
  • Estimated delivery date

Returns & Refunds Policy

Return Eligibility

Returns are accepted only in the following cases:

  • Manufacturing Defects: Identified within 7 days of delivery
  • Wrong Product Shipped: Due to error from our end
  • Transit Damage: With proper documentation and photos
  • Missing Components: Kit delivered incomplete

Non-Returnable Items

  • Products damaged due to improper installation
  • Products with tampered serial numbers or labels
  • Items used or installed
  • Custom-configured kits (unless defective)
  • Products returned after 7 days without prior approval

Return Process

  1. Initiate Return: Contact us within 7 days at info@suryagharkits.com or +91 9467643905
  2. Provide Details: Order number, reason for return, and supporting photos/videos
  3. Approval: Our team will review and approve eligible returns within 2 business days
  4. Return Authorization: You'll receive a Return Authorization Number (RAN)
  5. Packaging: Pack items securely in original packaging if possible
  6. Pickup/Shipping: We'll arrange pickup or provide return shipping instructions
  7. Inspection: Returned items will be inspected upon receipt
  8. Resolution: Refund or replacement processed within 7-10 business days

Refund Process

  • Refunds processed within 7-10 business days of receiving returned goods
  • Refund amount credited to original payment method
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable (except in case of wrong shipment or defective products)
  • Restocking fee of 15% may apply for approved returns (case-by-case basis)

Replacement Option

For defective products, we offer replacement at no additional cost:

  • Faster than refund process
  • No restocking fees
  • Free shipping for replacement
  • Priority processing

Damaged in Transit

If products are damaged during shipping:

  • Note damage on delivery receipt
  • Take photographs immediately
  • Contact us within 48 hours
  • Do not discard packaging materials
  • We'll coordinate with logistics partner for claim
  • Replacement or refund provided upon claim approval

Cancellation Policy

  • Before Dispatch: Full refund (processing fee may apply)
  • After Dispatch: Subject to return policy
  • Custom Orders: Non-cancellable after production begins
  • Cancellation requests: Email info@suryagharkits.com

Warranty Claims

For warranty-related issues after the 7-day return window, please contact us for manufacturer warranty claim processing. Warranty terms vary by component manufacturer.
