2. Our Kits (The Shop Page)
This is your e-commerce section. Functionality is key.
●	Page Title: Pre-Engineered Solar Kits for EPCs
●	Crucial Feature: Filters (on the left sidebar):
o	Kit Type: On-Grid, Off-Grid, Hybrid
o	System Capacity (kW): 1-3 kW, 3-5 kW, 5-10 kW, 10-25 kW, etc.
o	Panel Manufacturer: (Checkbox list of brands you offer)
o	Inverter Manufacturer: (Checkbox list of brands you offer)
o	Price Range Slider
●	Product Listing:
o	Each product is a card with:
▪	High-Quality Image of the kit/key components.
▪	Product Name: e.g., "10kW On-Grid Solar Kit | Waaree Panels & Sungrow Inverter"
▪	Price: "₹ [Price]"
▪	Key Specs Snippet: "445Wp Mono PERC Panels | 10kW 3-Phase Inverter"
▪	Buttons: [View Details] [Add to Cart]
●	Product Detail Page (When a user clicks 'View Details'):
o	Image Gallery
o	Product Title & Price
o	"What's in the Box?" Section: A detailed, itemized list.
▪	Solar PV Modules: Brand, Model, Wattage, Quantity (e.g., Waaree 540Wp Mono PERC x 19)
▪	Inverter: Brand, Model, Capacity (e.g., Sungrow 10kW 3-Phase On-Grid)
▪	Mounting Structure: Material (e.g., Hot-dip Galvanized Iron), Type (e.g., Designed for RCC Roof)
▪	Junction Boxes: AJB/DCDB specifications
▪	ACDB: Specifications
▪	Cables & Connectors: DC Cable length & size, AC Cable length & size, MC4 connectors quantity.
▪	Earthing & Lightning Arrestor: Included components.
o	Technical Datasheets Tab: Links to downloadable PDF datasheets for panels and inverter.
o	Warranty Tab: Clear information on the manufacturer warranty for each component.
o	Shipping & Delivery Tab: Estimated delivery timeframes by region.
o	Buttons: [Add to Cart] and a secondary [Add to Bulk Quote]
