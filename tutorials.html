Quick Tips & Best Practices

Optimal Panel Orientation

In India, solar panels should face south with a tilt angle equal to your latitude for maximum energy generation.

Proper Cable Sizing

Use 4mm² for up to 10A, 6mm² for 10-16A. Always calculate voltage drop to ensure less than 3% loss.

Safety First

Always use proper PPE, work with de-energized systems when possible, and follow lockout/tagout procedures.

Temperature Considerations

Panels lose 0.5% efficiency per °C above 25°C. Ensure proper ventilation and air gap under panels.

Waterproofing

Use marine-grade sealant on all roof penetrations. Check and reapply sealant annually during maintenance.

Battery Maintenance

Check battery water levels monthly for flooded batteries. Keep terminals clean and tight.
